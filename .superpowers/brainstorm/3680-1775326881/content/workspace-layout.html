Where the sheet music viewer sits during an active practice session

A: Full-Width Below Media

Sheet music gets its own full-width collapsible row between media and tools. When collapsed, it's a slim bar (title + "Page 3 of 12"). When expanded, the PDF fills the width — big enough to read comfortably. You can have media playing above while reading music below.

B: Side-by-Side with Media

Sheet music sits next to the media player in a 50/50 split. You see music and video at the same time without scrolling. Metronome moves down to the tools row. Tighter layout — everything visible at once, but each panel is smaller.

C: Stacked in Media Column

Sheet music stacks directly under the media player in the left column, keeping the metronome in its current spot on the right. Media column gets taller but the overall grid structure stays the same. Collapsible so it doesn't eat space when not needed.
