The flow

  • First time: "Upload New" or "From Library" buttons appear in the project card
  • After attach: Shows title + bookmark page, with an X to detach
  • Start Practicing: The sheet music auto-loads in the session viewer, already on your bookmarked page
  • One sheet, many projects: Same PDF can be attached to Guitar and Keys projects simultaneously
